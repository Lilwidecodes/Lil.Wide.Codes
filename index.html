Lil.Wide.Codes is a build-in-public engineering brand. The website serves as the central hub for documenting technical growth, showcasing projects, publishing engineering case studies, sharing development roadmaps (insights into the process and learnings), and demonstrating the evolution of software systems from idea to deployment.

WHY LIL.WIDE.CODES EXISTS

Why build publicly?

Because growth becomes more valuable when it is visible. Lil.Wide.Codes exists to show the process behind creating technology—not only the finished result. Public building creates accountability, records progress, and allows ideas to evolve through execution.

Why document?

Lessons disappear when they are not captured. Documentation is the bridge between creation and understanding. It preserves knowledge, accelerates learning, and ensures continuity in development.

Why create products?

Ideas become meaningful when people can use them. Products are the tangible outcomes of engineering efforts. They solve real problems, provide value to users, and serve as proof of concept.
